To-Do

How to use:

To use this app is really simple:

  • Type in a task in the box above and click the "Add" button to add it to your list
  • To edit a task, click the "Edit" button adjacent to the task you want to update
  • When you finish a task, click the "Complete" button to add it to your completed tasks list
  • To see your completed tasks, left-click on the text that says "Completed"
